# Project Toolkit & Skills Template

This template organizes all downloaded Skills, MCPs, and CLIs into functional categories. Use the **Project Recommendations** section at the bottom as a guide when spinning up new projects to ensure you activate the right stack.

---

## 🛠️ Skills Directory

### 🎨 Design & Brand Identity
*Core tools for establishing visual language, UI/UX, and component styling.*
*   **`brand`**: Defines brand voice, visual identity standards, and messaging hierarchy. Run first on any new brand.
*   **`brandkit`**: Outputs structured brand kit components (logo specs, palette, typography, spacing).
*   **`design-brief`**: Parses structured briefs before creating new assets, forcing explicit visual decisions.
*   **`design-system`**: Establishes reusable tokens (colors, type, spacing) for multi-channel consistency.
*   **`ui-ux-pro-max`**: Generates full design systems, UX guidelines, and pairs fonts/colors based on queries.
*   **`frontend-design`**: Forces bold, intentional aesthetic choices for production-grade UI.
*   **`ui-styling`**: Covers component implementation, Tailwind, dark mode, and responsive layouts.
*   **`design`**: Master design hub for generating logos, presentations, and icon assets.
*   **`taste-skill`**: Overrides default AI biases, ensuring premium design variance and motion intensity.
*   **`web-design-guidelines`**: Audits files for interface anti-patterns against Vercel design guidelines.
*   **`brutalist-skill`**: Applies Swiss Industrial/Tactical Terminal aesthetics (extreme typography, scanning, raw data).
*   **`minimalist-skill`**: Applies premium utilitarian minimalism (editorial serif, geometric sans, macro-whitespace).

### 📈 Marketing & Content Production
*Tools for social media, copywriting, advertising, and email campaigns.*
*   **`social-carousel`**: Generates 3-panel cinematic carousel series for Instagram.
*   **`social-content`**: Full social engine for hooks, captions, Reels scripts, and content calendars.
*   **`copywriting`**: Brand-voice copywriting across all formats with compliance constraints.
*   **`image-poster`**: Generates single-image print-ready posters and key art.
*   **`magazine-poster`**: Creates editorial-style posters (oversized headlines, 2-column body).
*   **`ad-creative`**: Builds compliant ad creative concepts for paid social (Meta/Instagram).
*   **`email-marketing`**: Designs brand product-launch emails (HTML layouts, mastheads, CTAs).

### 💻 Web Development, Digital & Motion
*Tools for prototyping, motion graphics, and advanced web experiences.*
*   **`impeccable`**: Deep UI/UX polish, auditing visual hierarchy, and implementing scroll-triggered animations.
*   **`web-prototype`**: Generates self-contained single-file HTML web prototypes for fast iteration.
*   **`site-architecture`**: Information architecture planning, URL structures, sitemaps, and navigation design.
*   **`video-shortform`**: Generates 3-10 second video clips, reveals, and ambient loops.
*   **`motion-frames`**: Creates looping CSS animations and single-frame motion compositions for digital displays.
*   **`webgpu-threejs-tsl`**: Builds GPU-accelerated 3D graphics, particle systems, and shaders in the browser.

### 🧠 Workflow & Infrastructure
*Tools for vault management and general research productivity.*
*   **`obsidian-markdown`**: Manages wikilinks, callouts, and frontmatter in Obsidian.
*   **`obsidian-bases`**: Builds database views and filtered collections.
*   **`json-canvas`**: Manages spatial layout of connected ideas in Obsidian Canvas.
*   **`obsidian-cli`**: Creates, reads, and searches notes via CLI.
*   **`defuddle`**: Scrapes clean markdown from web pages for research.
*   **`karpathy-guidelines`**: Enforces best practices: Think Before Coding, Simplicity First, Surgical Changes.
*   **`14-food-beverage`**: Specialized framework for lifestyle, sensory, and aspirational brand content.

## 🎯 Project-Based Toolkit Recommendations

When starting a new project, use these templates to decide which tools to activate.

### 1. Brand Identity & Strategy Sprint
**Goal**: Establish brand voice, visual guidelines, and core messaging.
*   **Primary Skills**: `brand`, `brandkit`, `design-brief`
*   **Supporting Skills**: `ui-ux-pro-max`, `taste-skill`
*   **MCPs**: Firecrawl (Competitor audit), Google Maps (Local landscape analysis)
*   **CLIs**: Ruflo CLI (Planning), Obsidian CLI (Documentation)

### 2. Website Design & Development
**Goal**: Build a premium, motion-rich, and high-converting web presence.
*   **Primary Skills**: `site-architecture`, `frontend-design`, `impeccable`, `ui-styling`
*   **Supporting Skills**: `taste-skill`, `webgpu-threejs-tsl` (for hero VFX), `web-design-guidelines` (for QA)
*   **MCPs**: Playwright (UI testing & audits)
*   **CLIs**: SkillUI CLI (Inspiration extraction), GitHub CLI, Node & npx

### 3. Social Media & Content Campaign
**Goal**: Produce high-quality, engaging social content and collateral.
*   **Primary Skills**: `social-content`, `social-carousel`, `video-shortform`
*   **Supporting Skills**: `copywriting`, `image-poster`, `14-food-beverage`
*   **MCPs**: Kie AI (Image/Video generation)
*   **CLIs**: Ruflo CLI (Content research swarm)

### 4. Paid Ad & Email Marketing Push
**Goal**: Drive conversion through targeted ads and email blasts.
*   **Primary Skills**: `ad-creative`, `email-marketing`, `copywriting`
*   **Supporting Skills**: `minimalist-skill` (for clean landing pages)
*   **MCPs**: Firecrawl (Influencer/competitor ad monitoring)
*   **CLIs**: Claude CLI

### 5. In-Store / Digital Display Assets (Retail)
**Goal**: Create looping, premium visual experiences for physical locations.
*   **Primary Skills**: `motion-frames`, `magazine-poster`, `webgpu-threejs-tsl`
*   **Supporting Skills**: `taste-skill`, `design-system`
*   **MCPs**: Kie AI (Raw asset generation)
*   **CLIs**: GitHub CLI (Asset versioning)

---
> [!TIP]
> **Pro Tip:** Always begin projects by running the `brand` and `design-brief` skills to lock in constraints. Use `taste-skill` as your constant quality gate to prevent generic "AI slop" from making it into your final deliverables.
