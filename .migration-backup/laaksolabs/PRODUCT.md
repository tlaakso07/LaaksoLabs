# Laakso Labs — Command Center

## Users
Single user: Trevor Laakso, founder. ADHD. Scans this dashboard multiple times daily — on desktop at his desk, on phone between meetings. Needs to know in under 5 seconds: what is my MRR, what must I do today, and is each client on track.

## Product Purpose
A private business command center for a three-division AI services company targeting $200K MRR. Tracks clients across a CRM pipeline, tasks, white-label partner orders (Happy Dog Media Group), revenue entries, and campaign performance. Not a public SaaS — it is a personal mission control room.

## Register
Product (design serves the data — clarity, speed, trust).

## Brand
**Laakso** (Finnish) = valley. Brand interpretation: "Lockso" — lock = trust, security, locking in results. Lab = cutting-edge, experimental, AI-first. The product should feel premium, purposeful, and like a serious operator built it — not a generic startup template.

## Anti-references
- Generic SaaS cream/white dashboards (Linear, Notion clones)
- Purple-gradient AI tool aesthetic
- Hollow startup energy ("Elevate your workflow")
- Cold, sterile fintech blue-gray
- Cluttered cockpit dashboards (too many numbers at once)

## Strategic Principles
1. ADHD-first: one primary action per view, bold hierarchy, color codes status instantly
2. Revenue obsession: $200K MRR target is always visible — it is the mission
3. Real data only: no fake numbers, no placeholder states that look like features
4. Premium feel: this is a tool for a serious business, it should feel like one
5. Speed: optimistic UI, no spinners, data-forward
